Deepfake Precautions

Common Warning Signs

  • Unnatural eye movement: Lack of blinking or strange eye movements
  • Facial distortions: Blurring or warping around the face, especially when moving
  • Inconsistent lighting: Shadows or highlights that don't match the environment
  • Audio-visual mismatch: Lip movements that don't sync perfectly with speech
  • Unnatural posture: Stiff or awkward head and body movements

Verification Tools

  • Reverse image search: Use Google Images or TinEye to find original sources
  • Metadata analysis: Check EXIF data for inconsistencies
  • Forensic analysis tools: Use tools like FotoForensics or InVID
  • Blockchain verification: Some platforms use blockchain to verify authentic media

Best Practices

  • Be skeptical of emotionally charged content
  • Verify information from multiple trusted sources
  • Check the date and context of the media
  • Look for official confirmations or debunks
  • Educate yourself and others about deepfake technology
